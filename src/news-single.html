Raya Achievers Club The Road To Vienna

Since 2001, each year Raya recognises its outstanding employees by Raya Achievers Club (RAC), in which the top performing employees across Lines of Business are rewarded with a trip to a country with exotic destinations in order to motivate and appreciate their work performance and efforts. For the year 2017, Vienna, Europe’s Cultural Capital, was the destination for the achievers of Raya group, where they had the chance to spend 5 days in Vienna to experience its flair, unique charm and vibrancy. Raya Contact Centre was proud of its achievers and that around 80 of its own employees were chosen to participate on this trip. The achievers had the opportunity to explore the city of culture by visiting one of the most visited attractions the Schoenbrunn place. Hungary is known for its magical nature because of Salzburg and the achievers had the chance to go to Salzburg for a whole day to experience the nature. There was a schedule for each day where the achievers had different fun activities planned that they participated in to enjoy the maximum from their trip and get to know each other. Raya takes pride in acknowledging employee’s efforts and will relentlessly find more opportunities to give back to the people who work hard day in and day out.
